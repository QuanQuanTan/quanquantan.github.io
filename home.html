About me

I am a third year PhD student from the Division of Mathematical Sciences, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore under the supervision of Thomas Peyrin. For my PhD programme, my main research focus is in cryptanalysis of symmetric key primitives.

Previously, I have also completed my B.Sc. in Mathematical Sciences, specializing in Applied Mathematics as well as a M.Sc.(Research) in Mathematical Sciences from NTU. The main focus of my master research was in the optimization techniques in the implementation of block ciphers.
